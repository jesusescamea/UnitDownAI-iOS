This is not a coding ticket. This is a product direction change.

After reviewing the Job Preview and Service Record Preview, I believe we are optimizing the wrong thing.

The current implementation feels like a report generator.

I want UnitDown to become the technician's operating system while they are on the roof.

The report is simply the result of everything captured naturally during the repair.

====================================================================

NEW DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

The technician should almost never think:

"I need to write a report."

Instead they should think:

"I'm simply documenting my work."

Everything they capture should automatically build the final professional report.

The timeline is the single source of truth.

Every action becomes a timeline event.

Voice.

Photos.

Measurements.

Parts.

Recommendations.

Verification.

Everything.

====================================================================

JOB MODE MUST BECOME THE PRIMARY EXPERIENCE

Do NOT think of Job Mode as another page.

Think of Job Mode as the active workspace that stays alive during the entire repair.

Once a technician taps Start Job, they remain inside that workflow until the repair is complete.

The report is generated AFTER the job.

Not during it.

====================================================================

REDESIGN GOALS

Instead of trying to make Job Mode look like paperwork...

Make it feel like a digital field notebook.

Fast.

Visual.

Minimal typing.

Everything captured in chronological order.
