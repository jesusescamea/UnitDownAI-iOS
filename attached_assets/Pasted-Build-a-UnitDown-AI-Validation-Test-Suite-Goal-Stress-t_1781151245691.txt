Build a UnitDown AI Validation Test Suite.

Goal:
Stress-test the diagnostic engine against 15 common HVAC issues with known expected outcomes.

Do not modify the diagnostic engine yet.

For each test:

* Submit the symptom
* Record the diagnosis
* Record confidence
* Record likely causes
* Compare to expected result
* Mark PASS / PARTIAL PASS / FAIL

TEST CASES

1. Low suction pressure + high superheat + low subcooling
   Expected:
   Undercharge / refrigerant leak

2. Low suction pressure + low superheat
   Expected:
   Airflow issue, low load, overfeeding TXV, floodback risk

3. High head pressure + high subcooling
   Expected:
   Overcharge or liquid-line restriction

4. High head pressure + low subcooling
   Expected:
   Condenser airflow issue or non-condensables

5. Evaporator coil freezing with dirty filter
   Expected:
   Airflow restriction

6. Compressor pulls LRA and breaker trips immediately
   Expected:
   Locked rotor, failed compressor, capacitor issue

7. 24V at thermostat Y call but no voltage at contactor coil
   Expected:
   Open control circuit, safety, relay, wiring issue

8. Jumping R to Y1 causes controls to drop out immediately
   Expected:
   Low-voltage control fault, transformer loading, wiring fault

9. Flame lights then drops out after a few seconds
   Expected:
   Flame sensor issue

10. Inducer runs but ignition never occurs
    Expected:
    Pressure switch, ignition module, safety chain issue

11. Condenser fan running backwards after motor replacement
    Expected:
    Motor wiring/phasing issue

12. Economizer stuck open bringing in hot air
    Expected:
    Economizer actuator/control fault

13. Capacitor tests good but motor hums and won't start
    Expected:
    Motor winding/mechanical failure

14. Unit cools for 15 minutes then trips low-pressure switch
    Expected:
    Low charge, icing, airflow, restriction investigation

15. Blower receives G call but relay never energizes
    Expected:
    Control board, relay, wiring fault

SCORING

PASS:
Primary diagnosis matches expected fault family.

PARTIAL PASS:
Expected fault appears in top 3 likely causes.

FAIL:
Expected fault does not appear or diagnosis is unrelated.

OUTPUT

Create a report table:

Test #
Input
Expected Diagnosis
Actual Diagnosis
Confidence
Pass/Partial/Fail
Notes

Then summarize:

* Overall accuracy %
* Electrical/control accuracy %
* Refrigeration accuracy %
* Airflow accuracy %
* Combustion accuracy %

List:

* Most common false positives
* Most common false negatives
* Patterns where the matcher is choosing the wrong tree

Do not modify code during testing.
Only generate the validation report first.
