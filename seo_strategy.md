# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Homepage (`/`)
- Public troubleshooting content (`/guides`, `/guides/:slug`, `/brand-guides`, `/brand-guides/:slug`)
- Public marketing/support pages (`/sponsor`, `/terms`, `/privacy`, `/legal`)

## Out of scope
- Authenticated account area (`/account`)
- Diagnostic records and logs (`/records/**`, `/logs/**`)
- Admin/internal routes (`/admin`)
- Login/signup flows except when they affect public crawlability or shared metadata

## Target audience
- HVAC technicians
- Facility / maintenance teams
- Commercial building owners and operators seeking troubleshooting guidance

## Primary keywords
- Commercial HVAC diagnostics
- Commercial HVAC troubleshooting
- Rooftop unit troubleshooting
- Brand-specific HVAC fault guides
- HVAC lockout causes and reset guides
